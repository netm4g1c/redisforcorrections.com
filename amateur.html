Repeaters

The following organizations sponsor amateur radio and GMRS repeaters in the Tippecanoe County area:

  • Western Indiana Amateur Radio Emergency Service (WI9RES)
    • WI9RES 145.370-, Rossville Wide Area, TSQL 131.8 Hz
    • WI9RES 147.135+, West Lafayette, TSQL 131.8 Hz, SKYWARN
    • WI9RES 443.500+, Rossville Wide Area, TSQL 131.8 Hz
  • Purdue Amateur Radio Club (W9YB)
  • W9ARP
  • West Lafayette GMRS

For more information on these repeater systems, please click on the link for each organization or contact them individually
